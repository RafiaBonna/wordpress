== Description ==

The Jewellery Boutique WordPress Theme is an elegant, feature-rich website template crafted for jewellery designers, boutique stores, and fine jewellery retailers looking to build a strong online presence. Ideal for showcasing everything from gold jewellery and diamond engagement rings to handmade artisan jewellery and affordable fashion accessories, this theme combines visual sophistication with powerful e-commerce features. Its clean, modern layout includes customizable product grids, lookbooks, and high-resolution image galleries that bring out the brilliance of gemstone rings, silver bracelets, pearl earrings, and more. Whether you're selling bespoke jewellery, stacking rings, or statement necklaces, this theme ensures every piece shines. Designed for WooCommerce, the theme supports features like quick product previews, secure checkout, wishlist functionality, and customer reviews. It's mobile-optimized and SEO-friendly, allowing potential buyers to easily browse wedding bands, cocktail rings, and minimalist jewellery collections across devices. With built-in sections for featured collections like bridal sets, birthstone jewellery, or custom engraved pieces, store owners can tailor their boutique to any niche—from ethical jewellery and recycled gold to men's chains and kids’ earrings. The theme also includes blog support for jewellery care tips, styling guides, or latest trends, enhancing engagement and boosting organic reach. Whether you’re running a luxury jewellery brand or launching your first handmade jewellery shop, this theme blends beauty with functionality.

== Installation ==
	
1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
